Perhaps something majestic
Like a lion in no way domestic

Perhaps something sublime
Like the Niagara in her prime

Perhaps something exhilarating
Like the merry-go-round rotating

Perhaps something inspiring
Like a mama bear protecting her cubs, untiring

Perhaps something pretty
Like the flower garden if let be

Or something musical
Like that of the birds in unison

Being spoilt for choice
Is no cause to rejoice

For being stuck in this indecision
Is much kind to vision
But in terms of execution
There is no provision!
