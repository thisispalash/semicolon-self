I remember that day, with the sun peeking
from behind the clouds, like your room from behind
you. A ray through the clear window aligns
just so the light fills my vision; you, gleaming

with your twinkling eyes and wide smiles, speaking
singularly; speaker view leaving me blind!
In the trusty rectangle, so unconfined,
your laugh guilds my heart, playfully teasing.

Twice a week your face pops up on my screen
Each week, as this is one class I can't miss!!
And each time you'd be there like mescaline

Transporting me to dimension psychédélique
making a hundred minutes seemingly remiss
leaving just your image, bright and angelic!
