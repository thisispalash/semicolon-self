The world is large
and I am not.
Maybe 'tis i
who has forgot.

Or perhaps not
for there are over
seven billion people (!)
And i am just one.

And those, just alive
There are billions more dead!
All those stories..
Will they ever be said?

Where is my story?
My poem? My song?
Is there even one?
Or am i just forlorn?

Maybe i etch myself
in other folk's lore;
leave my semblance
as colour, in their core.

Like a ghost, like Casper
Benign, not big spook
You may see me
If you know how to look

Piece my story together
Breathe life into it
Watch it enthrall
and charm and lure

For the world is large and I am not
But seven billion parts
in seven billion people
leaves me in this large world
For eternity!
